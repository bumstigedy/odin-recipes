Butter Frosting

Description

Mmm good! Cream cheese frosting. You can put this on any dessert!

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup granular sucrolose sweetener (such as Splenda®), or more to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat cream cheese and sucralose sweetener together in a bowl using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. .
  2. stir in whipped topping and vanilla extract until smooth
