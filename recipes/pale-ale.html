American Pale Ale

Description

A crisp and refreshing classic American Pale Ale. The bitterness from the hops is perfectly balanced with the maltiness from the grain.

Ingredients

  • 10 lbs Maris Otter
  • 1 oz Chinook hops
  • Wyeast 1056

Steps

  1. Mash at 152F for 60 mins
  2. Add Hops and boil for 60 mins
  3. Chill to 67F
  4. Pitch yeast and ferment until complete
